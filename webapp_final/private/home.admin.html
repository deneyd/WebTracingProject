Covid-19 information

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. Most people infected with the COVID-19 virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment. Older people, and those with underlying medical problems like cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, and cancer are more likely to develop serious illness.

PRECAUTIONS

Ways to prevent the spread of the disease

Hand Hygiene
Wash hands for at least 20 seconds or more with soap. Otherwise make use of hand sanitizer.
Use Face Masks
Use government certified face masks in public (N-95).
Avoid Touching
Avoid the touching of your face, nose or eyes.
Stay Home
Stay home when you feel unwell. Avoid participating in large social gatherings. Stay home whenever neccesary.

SYMPTOMS

Coronavirus Symptoms

The symptoms of coronavirus can take approximately up to 14 days to appear. The main symptoms are:

Vomit/Cough

If you are having dry coughs or an upset stomach/feeling nausea, this may be a sign of Covid-19.

Fever

Any temperature above 37.5 is considered to be infectious.

Cold/Body Aches

If you have a cold or any body aches, consult with your GP.
